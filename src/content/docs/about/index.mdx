    <h2>What you can do with Minish</h2>
    <ul class="about-capabilities">
      <li><strong>Embed at speed:</strong> embed the entire English Wikipedia in minutes on commodity hardware.</li>
      <li><strong>Classify on CPU:</strong> run document classification at tens of thousands of texts per second.</li>
      <li><strong>Curate large datasets:</strong> deduplicate and clean large datasets quickly with semantic tooling.</li>
      <li><strong>Build lightweight retrieval:</strong> power fast retrieval systems without large embedding stacks.</li>
      <li><strong>Compare ANN backends:</strong> evaluate which nearest-neighbor approach works best for your own data.</li>
